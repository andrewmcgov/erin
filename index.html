Erin Pickering

Cellist, Pianist, Event coordinator

About

An active member of the Ottawa music community, Erin has dedicated much of her professional life and education to music performance. Having studied and performed across Canada and internationally, Erin has gained valuable experience and skills with varying genres of music. Erin holds a Master of Music from the University of Ottawa and a Bachelor of Music from Western University, both in cello performance, and has also completed all levels of Conservatory Canada training in piano.

Services

Brighten your event with live music! Whether you are looking for background music at an office party or the perfect song to accompany you down the aisle on your special day, I provide beautiful music tailored to your requests.

Solo

If you are looking for an elegant, intimate musical experience for your event, the rich sound of the solo cello or piano is the perfect choice.

Ensemble

I have worked with many talented performers in Ottawa as a part of various duos, trios, quartets and more. If you are looking to book an ensemble of musicians, just ask! The following are types of ensembles you can request:

  • Cello Duo
  • Piano and cello duo
  • Piano and violin duo
  • Piano trio: piano, violin, and cello
  • Violin and cello duo

Whether you have specific song requests or just an idea of what genre of music you wish to have played, I am happy to work with you to perfectly capture what you have in mind.
